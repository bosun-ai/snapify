FAQ

  • What is ForSURE?

    ForSURE is a comprehensive software solution designed to help retailers, particularly small and medium enterprises (SMEs), manage their Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) compliance efficiently. It automates the complexities of EPR, making compliance straightforward and stress-free.

  • How does ForSURE help with EPR reporting?

    ForSURE automates the entire EPR reporting process, from data collection to report submission. It drastically reduces the time spent on compliance, enabling businesses to generate accurate reports in minutes. For more details, you can read our EPR software page.

  • Who can benefit from using ForSURE?

    ForSURE is ideal for retailers, e-commerce businesses, importers, and manufacturers who need to comply with EPR regulations. Its especially beneficial for SMEs looking for a streamlined solution to manage their EPR obligations without dedicating excessive resources.

  • What EPR areas does ForSURE cover?

    ForSURE covers a wide range of EPR obligations, including Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE), batteries, packaging (SUP), and textiles. The software ensures compliance with multiple regulatory frameworks across different regions, making it a versatile tool for businesses.

  • How can I get started with ForSURE?

    Getting started with ForSURE is simple. You can explore the software's features directly on the website or schedule a demo to see how it can meet your specific EPR compliance needs. The platform is designed to be user-friendly, making it easy to begin managing your EPR obligations efficiently. If you are eligible sign up for a free plan!
